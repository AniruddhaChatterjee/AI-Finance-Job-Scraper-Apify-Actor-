# AI-Finance-Job-Scraper-Apify-Actor-
AI Finance Job Scraper (Apify Actor)

An intelligent job scraping and enrichment tool designed specifically for finance-focused roles across multiple job platforms.

This Actor collects, filters, and enriches job listings using AI to help candidates, analysts, and job platforms discover high-quality finance opportunities faster.

🚀 Features
🔍 Multi-Platform Scraping
Scrapes jobs from platforms like LinkedIn, Indeed, company career pages, and startup boards
🎯 Finance-Focused Filtering
Targets roles in:
Investment Banking
Equity Research
FP&A
Corporate Finance
Private Equity / Venture Capital
Risk & Compliance
Accounting & Audit
🤖 AI-Powered Classification
Categorizes jobs into finance domains
Detects seniority level (Analyst, Associate, Manager, etc.)
Identifies required skills (Excel, SQL, Valuation, etc.)
🧠 Smart Insights
Extracts key information:
Salary (if available)
Experience required
Skills & tools
Generates job summaries
🌍 Location Intelligence
Supports:
India (Mumbai, Bangalore, etc.)
Remote roles
Global finance hubs
📊 Structured Output
Clean JSON output ready for:
Dashboards
Notion / Airtable
Analytics pipelines
